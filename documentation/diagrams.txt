[Flowchart of Simple Banking System]

Start
  |
  v
Display Menu:
1. Deposit
2. Withdraw
3. Check Balance
4. Exit
  |
  v
User selects option
  |
  +--> Deposit:
  |       Enter amount
  |       Update balance
  |       Show new balance
  |       Back to Menu
  |
  +--> Withdraw:
  |       Enter amount
  |       If balance >= amount
  |           Deduct amount
  |           Show new balance
  |       Else
  |           Show "Insufficient balance"
  |       Back to Menu
  |
  +--> Check Balance:
  |       Display current balance
  |       Back to Menu
  |
  +--> Exit:
          End Program
